How NEOBET AI Works

Our quantum-powered prediction system follows a rigorous 4-stage process to deliver the most accurate football forecasts available anywhere.

1. Data Aggregation

We collect real-time data from over 42 proprietary sources:

  • Player tracking systems (GPS, accelerometer data)
  • Opta and StatsBomb event streams
  • Satellite weather tracking
  • Bookmaker odds movements
  • Social media sentiment analysis

2. Quantum Processing

Our quantum neural network processes this data through:

  • 128-qubit processing for complex probability calculations
  • Topological data analysis to identify hidden patterns
  • Monte Carlo simulations with 10,000+ iterations per match
  • Bayesian inference models updated in real-time

3. Prediction Generation

The system outputs multiple prediction types with confidence scores:

  • Match Outcome: Win/Draw/Lose probabilities
  • Correct Score: Most likely scorelines
  • Goal Markets: Over/Under, BTTS, timing
  • Player Props: Goalscorers, cards, assists

4. Human Verification

All premium predictions undergo final review by our team of professional traders who:

  • Apply market context to AI outputs
  • Identify value discrepancies with bookmaker odds
  • Ensure optimal staking strategies
  • Provide clear reasoning for each recommendation
